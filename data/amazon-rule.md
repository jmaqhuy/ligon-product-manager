# Amazon Listing Guidelines

## 1. Product Title Requirements and Guidelines
The product title or product name (the **Item name** field on the **List your products** page) is the first piece of information that customers see when they search for a product on Amazon. Titles must clearly and concisely describe key product information. Clear titles help customers to identify, differentiate, and compare products, which simplifies their decision-making process and increases the likelihood of sale.

### Product title requirements
To list a product for the first time, your title must meet the requirements described below:

| Requirement type | Details |
|---|---|
| **Character limit** | Titles must not exceed 75 characters, including spaces. |
| **Promotional content** | Titles must not contain promotional phrases, such as "free shipping", "100% quality guaranteed", "best seller", "hot deal", or "sale". |
| **Special characters** | Titles must not use the following special characters: `!`, `$`, `?`, `_`, `{`, `}`, `^`, `¬`, `¦`. Other special characters, such as `~`, `#`, `<`, `>`, and `*`, are allowed only in specific contexts. For example, you may use these symbols as product identifiers ("Style #4301") or measurements ("<10 lb"). Decorative usage of special characters is not allowed. |
| **Content requirements** | Titles must contain the minimum amount of information that can be used to clearly describe the product. Do not include restricted phrases, such as "FSA/HSA eligible". Do NOT use Emojis. |
| **Word repetition** | Titles must not contain the same word more than twice. Prepositions, articles, and conjunctions may be used more than twice in a title. |

### Best practices for Titles
- **Length:** Keep titles concise (maximum 75 characters).
- **Redundancy:** Avoid repeating words or information.
- **Information order:** Brand name, flavor or style, product type, key attribute, color, size or pack count, model number.
- **Numbers:** Use numerals instead of spelling out numbers (e.g., "2" instead of "two").
- **Subjective commentary:** Don't use subjective phrases like "Hot Item" or "Best seller".
- **Capitalization:** Don't use all caps. Capitalize the first letter of each word, except for prepositions, conjunctions, or articles.
- **Abbreviations:** Measurements can be abbreviated (cm, oz, in, kg, mm).

---

## 2. Item Highlights
When your title is kept to the 75-character limit, you can add item highlights. Item highlights provide an additional 125 characters for product details like materials or recommended use cases. These should be written as comma-separated phrases, instead of full sentences. Maximum 125 characters total.

---

## 3. Product Descriptions
Give a concise, honest, and friendly overview of its uses and where it fits in its category. Discuss the features and benefits of the product and focus on your product's unique properties. Don't mention competitors. Highlight the best applications for the product.
- **Character length:** Between 1000 and 2000 characters.
- **Formatting:** Do not use HTML, JavaScript, or code. As a special exception, you can use `<br>` line breaks in the description.
- **Prohibited content:** No phone numbers, email addresses, URLs, availability details, price info, review requests, or promotional claims.

---

## 4. Bullet Points
Bullet points are concise statements that highlight key product features and benefits in order to help customers make informed purchasing decisions.
- **Bullet point count:** Exactly five bullet points.
- **Character length:** Maximum 255 characters per bullet point (recommended 150-255 characters).
- **Capitalization:** Begin with a capital letter.
- **Formatting:** Format as a sentence fragment, and don't use end punctuation. Use semicolons to separate phrases within a single bullet point.
- **Structure:** Add a header with a colon followed by a description (example: "Cotton fabric: Made from 100% cotton").
- **Prohibited elements:** NO Emojis (😊, ✅, etc.), NO special symbols (™, ®, €, …, †, ‡), NO promotional claims (free shipping, 100% guarantee), NO subjective claims (best gift ever, #1 rated), NO external links or contacts.

---

## 5. Search Tags and Slugs (SEO)
- **Tags:** Provide 15 to 25 highly relevant search terms and keywords. Do NOT include semicolons within an individual tag keyword. The total joined length of all tags separated by semicolons must not exceed 500 characters.
- **Slugs:** Provide 12 distinct SEO keyword phrases describing the item for image file naming. These will be automatically converted to lowercase hyphenated slugs by the backend.
